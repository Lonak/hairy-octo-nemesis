What's inside?

Features:

  • Loads the French Rappelz News with a GMT time stamp;
  • Responsive ads at the bottom of the screen to provide some very low beer money to the developer!
  • That's pretty much it for now.

 

Upcoming features:

  • Complete overhaul from hybrid HTML5/Native build to a complate native build to provide speed boost and enhanced experience;
  • Native RSS to HTML script. (embedding through RSSDog for now);
  • Translation in different languages;
  • Selection by region of the Rappelz news (USA, France, Germany, Poland, Italy, Turkey) to make a one app for all rappelzians;
  • Push notifications on a new official article post;
  • Update by category (Cash Shop, Events, General news, Maintenances, Patch Notes, ...);
  • Direct access to official links (YouTube, website, forums, etc.)
